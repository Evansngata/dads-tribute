About Him

My dad, Jeremiah Njenga, was a man of love, wisdom, and strength. Born and raised in Kiambu, Kenya, he was a family man and my inspiration. He was calm in how he acted and every moment being around him was great.

Whether it was watching games with him, or going on errands with him, he somehow knew how to make me smile. His laughter, personality and structure shaped me into who I am now.
